About Me

Hello, I'm Livia! I am a senior at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln double majoring in journalism and advertising/public relations and minoring in psychology. I have a strong passion for the written word and am looking to kick off my career in news reporting that brings truth and transparency to my community.

I am a news intern at the Lincoln Journal Star, where I report on breaking news topics and feature stories. The best part of this role is getting to meet incredible Lincolnites with special stories, all while learning from experienced journalists who care deeply about local journalism.

Previously a coordinator for the Intern Foundry program, I directly tackled Nebraska's brain drain challenge by connecting high-achieving students with purpose-driven, project-based internships.
