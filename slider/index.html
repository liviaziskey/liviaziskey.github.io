UNL Campus Construction: 2014 vs. 2025

Welcome to the UNL Campus Construction slider! Use this interactive tool to visualize the changes and developments on the University of Nebraska-Lincoln campus between two different time periods.

How to Use:

  • Desktop: Click and drag the vertical white bar (the "slider handle") left or right to reveal more of one image or the other.
  • Mobile: Touch and drag the vertical white bar left or right with your finger to achieve the same effect.

The image on the left represents an earlier view of the campus (2014), while the image on the right shows a later view (2025, hypothetical for this example). Observe the differences in buildings, landscaping, and overall layout as you move the slider!
