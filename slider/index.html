Social Science Hall to Temple Building: A Century of Change

Explore the evolution of one of UNL's most elegant buildings. Completed in 1919 as the Social Sciences Hall, this iconic structure with its grand Ionic columns was a cornerstone of campus expansion. Today, it is the Temple Building, home to the Johnny Carson School of Theatre and Film. Drag the slider to compare its original 1920s appearance to its 2025 look.
